# NEXGEN EXECUTIVES OS – Product Requirements Document

## Vision
An autonomous executive operating system designed to transform organizational knowledge into executive action.

## Core Capabilities
- Executive Intelligence Dashboard
- Project Portfolio Management
- Strategic Task Tracking
- Meeting Intelligence
- Decision Registry
- AI Executive Assistant
- Organizational Knowledge Layer

## Technology Stack
- FastAPI
- MongoDB
- React
- Gemini
- MCP
- TailwindCSS

## Target Users
- Chief Executive Officer
- Executive Vice Presidents
- Business Unit Directors
- Executive Follow-up Officers
- System Administrators

## Objective
Provide a unified executive operating environment for strategic planning, monitoring, decision-making, and organizational execution.
